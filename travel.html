Upper Pennisula Michigan, July 2020

It was last minute planned mother and daughter trip to go up north to the U.P. A fun and memorable trip, my sister overcame her fear of driving on the freeway. The amazing view of driving along the shore of Lake Michigan. First stop was to go see Picture Rocks National Lakeshore and waterfalls chasing. Afterward to our destination, Mackinac Island. An island that's surround by Lake Huron; the island travel traffic is by foot, bike, or horse. A fantastic ferry ride to arrive and depart the island. Definitely a must visit location!




Clearwater, FL February 2019

I remember getting a phone call from my sister, saying that her and my cousin found a good deal on a round trip flight to Florida and if I wanted to tag along. On a whimp, I said yes, I haven't travel there before and at the time I needed getaway. I was excited because I needed a break and I haven't been there before and "Hello" who wouldn't be excited to go to Florida and go be a beach bum?? The Airbnb that we stayed at was aesthically gorgeous. Weather was beautiful, no rains! Me and sister were on a murals and dolphin statues hunt. I'd jetski for the first time in my life. The clarity of the water is where "Clearwater" gets it name from.
